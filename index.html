WELCOME TO EARTH SMILES COMPOST

Earth Smiles Compost is a company that specializes in providing compost services. We collect organic waste from residential and commercial customers, process it into nutrient-rich compost, and offer it for sale to gardeners, landscapers, and environmentally conscious individuals and organizations. Our mission is to reduce waste, promote sustainability, and help our customers create healthier and more vibrant landscapes.

We make composting fun.

1. You throw away your food scarps in the container we provide.

2. We pick it up every week and swap it with a new container.

3. We give it your compost or share with our farmers partners, and they send you sample produce made with your own compost!
